Cookies

Introduction

cookies are a timeless favorite. Soft and chewy with pockets of melty chocolate chips, they're perfect for a quick snack, dessert, or bake sale.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Vanilla Exract
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt

Step to Make Cookies

  1. Cream the butter and sugar
  2. Add eggs and vanilla
  3. Add flour, baking powder, and salt
  4. Cover and chill the dough
